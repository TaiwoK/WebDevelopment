How to Boil Rice

  1. Perboil the rice for few minutes
  2. Wash the perbiled rice
  3. Add water in a pot and allow to boil
  4. Add the washed rice to the boiling water
  5. Add salt and little vegetable oil to improve taste
  6. Allow to cook for 30 minutes
  7. Serve with stew and enjoy
